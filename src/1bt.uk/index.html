Are you a developer exploring an awesome name for your personal website and projects? You came to the right place!

1bt.uk is a free identity for developers on the web. This service offers a handy subdomain service for you to have a subdomain like foo.1bt.uk or bar.1bt.uk for use on your static sites via CNAME, A, or AAAA DNS record. Fork, add subdomain and send a Pull Request. We will make it work!
